# Design System: Space-Based-Solar-Power.com

## 1. Design System Purpose

This design system governs the visual, spatial, typographic, motion, accessibility, and multilingual behavior of Space-Based-Solar-Power.com.

The design system must not create a generic technology interface.

It must support a thesis-driven sovereign interface that embodies orbital energy infrastructure, energy sovereignty, wireless power transmission, and future space-industrial systems.

## 2. Design Principle

The governing design principle is:

> The interface must not decorate the asset. The interface must embody the asset’s thesis.

Every visual decision must connect to at least one of the following:

- orbital energy collection;
- power beaming;
- terrestrial reception;
- grid resilience;
- AI energy demand;
- defense and disaster-response infrastructure;
- lunar and space-industrial extension;
- multilingual global authority;
- institutional trust.

## 3. Approved Visual Character

The approved visual character is:

- sovereign;
- spatial;
- embodied;
- infrastructural;
- precise;
- cinematic only when purposeful;
- institutional rather than entertainment-driven;
- deep-tech rather than consumer-tech;
- alive without becoming chaotic.

## 4. Rejected Visual Character

The project rejects:

- generic dark SaaS layouts;
- decorative sci-fi dashboards;
- gaming aesthetics;
- childish space visuals;
- random neon grids;
- crypto-style interface language;
- low-trust stock imagery;
- excessive glassmorphism;
- visual effects that do not serve meaning.
