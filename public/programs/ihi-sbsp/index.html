Industry Consortium

IHI Corporation, one of Japan's major industrial conglomerates with expertise in aerospace systems, power generation, and large-scale engineering, has engaged with space-based solar power as part of Japan's national SBSP industrial development framework. IHI's SBSP activities include participation in JAXA-coordinated research programmes, technology development for large space structures, and system integration studies. IHI's industrial capabilities in large structure fabrication and space systems engineering make it a relevant participant in any future Japanese SBSP development programme. IHI's SBSP engagement is at research and study phase, not development commitment.

Institutional Context

IHI Corporation is a diversified Japanese industrial corporation with significant activities in aerospace, energy, industrial machinery, and transportation infrastructure. IHI has existing contracts and relationships with JAXA and participates in Japanese government-coordinated aerospace research. Its engagement with SBSP occurs within the context of Japan's national SBSP industrial development strategy and METI's industry roadmap activities.

SBSP Relevance

IHI's SBSP engagement is relevant as an indicator of which Japanese industrial actors are participating in the national SBSP development framework. IHI's large structure engineering capabilities are directly applicable to SBSP solar array structure and in-space assembly challenges, making it a potentially significant industrial contributor to a future Japanese SBSP programme.

Technology Relationship

IHI SBSP research engages with solar array structural design, large space structure dynamics, in-space assembly, and modular spacecraft architecture as primary technology domains relevant to IHI's industrial engineering capabilities.

Feasibility Boundary

IHI's SBSP activities are at research and participation phase within Japan's national framework. No IHI-led SBSP development programme or funded production commitment has been confirmed.

Claim Boundary

IHI's SBSP engagement should be presented as industrial research participation, not as a development commitment or production capability. IHI's participation contributes to Japanese national SBSP research but does not constitute a programme of record.
