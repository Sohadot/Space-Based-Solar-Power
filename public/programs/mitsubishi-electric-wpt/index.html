Mitsubishi Electric Corporation has conducted wireless power transmission research directly relevant to space-based solar power, including microwave beaming demonstrations and phased array transmitter development. Mitsubishi Electric's WPT research represents some of the most technically advanced industry-led wireless power transmission work in Japan, contributing to the technology readiness base for SBSP microwave transmission subsystems. Mitsubishi Electric's SBSP-relevant research is conducted within the context of Japan's national SBSP programme and the company's broader activities in satellite communications and space systems. These activities are research phase, not development of an operational WPT or SBSP product.

Institutional Context

Mitsubishi Electric is one of Japan's leading electronics and electrical engineering corporations, with major activities in satellite communications, radar systems, and power electronics. The company's SBSP-relevant WPT research builds on its expertise in phased array antenna systems and power electronics, drawing on capabilities developed for satellite communications and radar applications. Mitsubishi Electric participates in JAXA-coordinated SBSP research and contributes to Japan's industrial SBSP capability development.

SBSP Relevance

Mitsubishi Electric's WPT research is among the most technically relevant industry contributions to SBSP microwave transmission subsystem development in Japan. The company's phased array and power electronics expertise directly addresses key technology gaps in the SBSP transmission subsystem, and its research outputs contribute to advancing TRL for microwave WPT at SBSP-relevant parameters.

Technology Relationship

Mitsubishi Electric SBSP-relevant research engages directly with phased array transmitter systems, microwave power transmission, beam steering, and power conversion and conditioning as primary technology domains.

Feasibility Boundary

Mitsubishi Electric's WPT research is at laboratory and demonstration scale. Research outputs demonstrate subsystem-level technical progress, not operational SBSP capability. No Mitsubishi Electric SBSP product development programme has been confirmed.

Claim Boundary

Mitsubishi Electric's WPT research should be cited as industry-led technology research contributing to SBSP subsystem TRL advancement, not as development of an operational WPT or SBSP product.
